Game dev philosophy

At super game factory, we not only make games. We aslo tell stories.

Fascinated by the surrealist univers, and the way it reflect people feelings, we deside to craft virtual worlds to express feeling, ideas and stories through games.

The interactive side of our game is the core of the experience, we try to think our game mechanics in a way of coerency with the story we want to tell.

We want to tell stuff by questioning the player on what he is currently playing, and what does it mean.
